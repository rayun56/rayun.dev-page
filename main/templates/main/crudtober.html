What is crudtober?

crudtober is the month-long challenge to do shit. Inspired by my friend David over at korio.me, the goal is to do something every day, rather than sit thinking about all the things you should be doing. A little one-off project here and there, or chipping away at a bigger project you know you gotta do.
My log will be below, and I'll try to update it daily. (As stated by David, feel free to try this out for yourself ^-^)
